Privacy Policy

App: Anime Print Color by Number
Effective date: April 28, 2026
Last updated: April 28, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Anime Print Color by Number ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app on your mobile device. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

The app does not require you to enter any personal information directly. Account creation is handled exclusively through Google Play Games sign-in, which provides us with the following information from your Google account:

  • Email address
  • Google account display name
  • Google Play Games player ID

1.2 Information collected automatically

When you use the app, the following data is collected and stored:

  • App activity: coloring progress, completed characters, achievement progress, gem and diamond balance, in-app preferences (sound, vibration, language)
  • Device and diagnostic data: device model, operating system version, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Identifiers: Firebase user ID, Google Advertising ID (Ad ID)
  • Purchase history: in-app purchases and subscription status (handled by Google Play Billing)

1.3 Information we do not collect

  • Precise or approximate location
  • Photos, videos, audio, or files from your device
  • Contacts, calendar entries, or messages
  • Health, fitness, or financial data
  • Phone numbers

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may receive or process information:

3.1 Google / Firebase

  • Firebase Authentication — manages your login session
  • Firebase Firestore — stores your cloud-saved progress
  • Firebase Cloud Storage — serves character image assets
  • Firebase Crashlytics — reports app crashes for debugging
  • Firebase Analytics — measures aggregate app usage
  • Firebase App Check (Play Integrity) — verifies that requests originate from a legitimate app instance
  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app purchases
  • Google Play Games Services — enables sign-in and (where applicable) achievements/leaderboards

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 CleverAdsSolutions (CAS) and ad partners

The app uses CleverAdsSolutions to show advertisements. CAS may share your Ad ID and limited device information with its mediation partners (which can include Google AdMob, AppLovin, Unity Ads, Meta Audience Network, and others) in order to serve ads.

CAS privacy policy: cleveradssolutions.com/privacy-policy

You can reset or limit your Ad ID at any time through your device's settings: Settings → Google → Ads.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as described below:

  • With Google services and CAS as listed in Section 3, strictly for the purposes of providing the app's functionality
  • When required by law (e.g., a valid legal request or court order)
  • To protect rights, safety, or to investigate fraud or abuse

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS / TLS connections. User data is stored on Google's Firebase infrastructure with access restricted to the authenticated user (enforced by Firestore security rules) and protected at rest by Google's standard encryption.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists
  • Crash and analytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention (typically up to 14 months for Analytics events; longer for Crashlytics)
  • Purchase records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with tax and accounting law

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — most of it is visible inside the app
  • Delete your account and associated data — see our Account Deletion page
  • Reset your Ad ID — through your device's Google settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads — through your device's Google settings ("Opt out of Ads Personalization")

If you are in the EU/EEA, UK, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA, including the right to object to processing or to receive a copy of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email below and we will delete the data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated through the app or via Google Play release notes.
