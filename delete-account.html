Account Deletion — Anime Print Color by Number

You can delete your account and all associated data using either of the methods below.

Option 1 — Delete inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Anime Print Color by Number on your device
  2. Tap the menu (top left) → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2 — Request deletion by email (if you've uninstalled the app)

Send an email to anvoan.art.easy@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request", and include the Google account email you used to sign in to the app.

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email when complete.

What gets deleted

  • Your saved characters and coloring progress
  • Your gem and diamond balance
  • Your profile data (Firebase Authentication account)
  • Error logs associated with your account

What may be retained

  • Anonymized aggregate analytics (e.g., total completions per character) — cannot be tied back to you
  • Purchase receipts and transaction records, retained as required by tax and accounting law (typically up to 7 years)
  • Backup copies, purged within 30 days of your deletion request
